# Walnut SEO Keyword Language

Use this vocabulary for homepage SEO, social previews, AI Growth emails, X campaigns, X replies, Reddit drafts, and social cards.

Primary search language:

- Congress trades
- Congressional stock trades
- Insider activity
- Insider trading tracker
- Stock research
- Ticker intelligence
- Market signals
- Institutional activity
- Government contracts
- Fundamentals
- Technicals
- Signal stack

Secondary/contextual language:

- Confirmation stack
- Confirmation signals
- Evidence trail
- Market tells
- Price/volume confirmation
- Reported holdings/activity

Rules:

- Lead with search phrases people already use; keep `confirmation stack` as supporting product language.
- Do not use `Confirmation-Stack Market Intelligence` as a homepage title, campaign headline, or primary hook.
- Do not use stale positioning such as `Market Intelligence from Political Trades and Insider Activity`.
- Use consistent X campaign language across post copy, card headlines, card chips, and visual briefs.
- Options Flow is still coming soon; do not describe it as an available Walnut feature until product status changes.

Good title/hook patterns:

- `Walnut Markets | Congress Trades & Insider Activity Research`
- `Congressional Stock Trades and Insider Signals`
- `Stock Research from Congress Trades, Insider Activity, and Market Signals`
